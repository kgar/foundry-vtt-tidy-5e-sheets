An alternate view of items that brings more functionality to the surface. The goal of Tidy's Item Sheet is to bring every piece of content that is most commonly referenced in an item up to the first screen. 

> [!TIP]
> New to Tidy sheets? Check out the [Getting Started](https://github.com/kgar/foundry-vtt-tidy-5e-sheets/wiki/1.-Getting-Started) guide to learn how to switch your item sheets to Tidy. For basic functionality like drag and drop, see [The Basics](https://github.com/kgar/foundry-vtt-tidy-5e-sheets/wiki/2.-The-Basics).

Tidy supports every system item type:

- Background
- Class
- Consumable
- Container
- Equipment
- Facility *(Requires 5e 2024 rules)*
- Feat
- Loot
- Species
- Spell
- Subclass
- Tattoo *(Requires TCoE module)*
- Tool
- Weapon

&nbsp;

# Item Sheet Sidebar

- Rarity colors and labels
- Spellcasting preparation mode colors and labels
- Equip / Unequip (Equipment items)
- Attune / Unattune (Magic items)
- Identify / Unidentify (in Edit Mode)
- Item details
- Currency (Container items)
